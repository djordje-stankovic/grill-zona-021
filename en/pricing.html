FAQ

Frequently asked questions about pricing

Are there any hidden costs?

No. The price you see is the price you pay. For Business and Premium packages, there's a monthly fee for hosting and maintenance — clearly stated upfront. No extra charges.

Why no contracts?

Because we believe in what we do. There's no need to lock you into any contract. If our service is good — you'll stay. If not — you're free to leave at any time. No pressure, no penalties.

Can I switch packages?

Yes, at any time. You can upgrade from Business to Premium or vice versa. No additional setup — only the monthly price changes. You can also switch to Starter and get your files to continue on your own.

What happens if I cancel?

Absolutely nothing. You get your website files and can use them wherever you want. Hosting is deactivated, but the website is yours. No penalties, no hidden costs.

How long does website creation take?

On average 5-10 business days from the agreement. For urgent projects, we can work faster — get in touch to discuss timelines.

What is a "custom report" in the Premium package?

You choose which information to track — site visits, Google position, site speed, Google profile statistics, or everything at once. The report arrives every 10 days to your email. You can change your metric selection anytime.

Can I see the website before paying?

YES. If we contacted you, you've already seen a finished proposal. If you came to us, we'll design a mockup you can review before committing.

What if I want something that's not in a package?

Get in touch with your request. We can add anything — from online orders to custom functionality. We provide an estimate upfront, no surprises.

Ready to get started?

Get in touch today — we'll design a proposal for your new website. No obligations.
